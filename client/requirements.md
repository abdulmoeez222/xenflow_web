## Packages
framer-motion | Complex animations and smooth scroll effects
lucide-react | Icons for UI elements
clsx | Utility for conditional classes (standard in shadcn)
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging tailwind classes (standard in shadcn)
react-hook-form | Form handling
@hookform/resolvers | Zod resolution for forms

## Notes
- Design aesthetic: Dark mode, futuristic, high-tech, black and neon red accents (#FF0000), glassmorphism.
- Fonts: 'Inter' (sans) and 'Space Mono' (monospace/display).
- Animations: Smooth scroll reveals, floating elements, hover effects using framer-motion.
- Images: Abstract 3D shapes/liquid metal placeholders (Unsplash) for visual interest.
